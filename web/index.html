Airdrop-rights NFT concept

Tracked in issue #15: wrap farm-cohort exposure as a transferable NFT so depositors can trade their pre-claim position.

1. Deposit USDC
Fund a cohort of wallets farming a specific protocol.
2. Mint rights NFT
Receive an ERC-721 representing your pro-rata share of future drops.
3. Trade or claim
Sell the NFT pre-claim or redeem it for your share of whatever drops.
